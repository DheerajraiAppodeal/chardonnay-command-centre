## Question Bank

### For Jordi (CPO)
- "What does good look like at the end of my first 90 days — in your words?"
- "What's the one thing the team struggles with that I should fix first?"
- "How do you prefer to receive updates — async Slack or weekly sync?"
- "When should I escalate vs. handle it myself?"
- "Are there any landmines I should know about before I start changing things?"

### For Product Managers (Didara, Srikanth, Giulia)
- "What takes the most time out of your week that has nothing to do with product decisions?"
- "What's the most frustrating coordination problem you're dealing with right now?"
- "If I could fix one thing about how Jira works for your team in 30 days, what would it be?"
- "Who do you depend on most outside your team, and how is that working?"
- "What would make you feel like this role is working well for you?"

### For Engineering Leads (Toni, Yevhenii)
- "What's the biggest process friction for your team right now?"
- "How well does Jira reflect what's actually happening in development?"
- "What do you wish PMs understood better about how your team works?"
- "What dependencies are hardest to track or communicate upward?"

### For QA (Krish)
- "What information do you most often have to hunt for before you can start testing?"
- "What's the most common reason a ticket comes to you unprepared?"
- "If tickets were consistently better, what would that save you each week?"

## After the 1:1 — Capture These

1. Their biggest pain (in their words if possible)
2. What they think success looks like for your role
3. Any names they mention repeatedly (signals who the influencers are)
4. Anything they're worried about — even if said indirectly
5. Any commitments you made → log immediately

---

## Signals to watch for across 1:1s

- **Same complaint from multiple people** → systemic problem, prioritise it
- **Nobody mentions a person** → they may be invisible or sidelined
- **Lots of praise for something** → don't change it without very good reason
- **Vague answers to direct questions** → follow up 1:1, not in group settings
- **"That's just how it's always been"** → process that no one owns, prime for improvement
